My Favourite Books

The books that helped me think and act.

  • A Tale of Two Cities  — Charles Dickens

    What is this book about

    "Set against the chaos of the French Revolution, this novel explores love, sacrifice, and redemption as lives in London and Paris intertwine. It’s a story of courage and the transformative power of selflessness."

  • To Kill a Mockingbird  — Harper Lee

    What is this book about

    "Through the eyes of young Scout Finch, the novel examines racial injustice, moral growth, and compassion in the American South. It is a timeless story about empathy, courage, and standing up for what is right."

  • Alchemist  — Paulo Coelho

    What is this book about

    "A shepherd named Santiago embarks on a journey to find his personal legend, discovering wisdom, love, and the interconnectedness of life along the way. The story is a meditation on following dreams and listening to one’s heart."

  • The Great Gatsby  — F. Scott Fitzgerald

    What is this book about

    "Set in the Roaring Twenties, the novel follows Jay Gatsby’s pursuit of love and the American Dream, revealing wealth, illusion, and moral decay. It is a tragic exploration of desire, ambition, and the passage of time."
